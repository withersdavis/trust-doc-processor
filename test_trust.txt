JERRY SIMONS 2012 TRUST

THIS TRUST AGREEMENT is made this 15th day of March, 2012, by and between JERRY SIMONS, as Grantor and Trustee.

ARTICLE I
TRUST ESTATE

The Grantor hereby transfers and delivers to the Trustee the property described in Schedule A attached hereto, receipt of which is hereby acknowledged by the Trustee, to have and to hold the same and any cash, securities, or other property which the Trustee may, pursuant to any of the provisions hereof, at any time hereafter hold or acquire, all of which property, together with the proceeds thereof, are herein collectively referred to as the "Trust Estate" or "Trust Property," for the uses and purposes and upon the terms and conditions herein set forth. The trusts hereunder may be referred to as the "JERRY SIMONS 2012 TRUST" or the "Trust."

ARTICLE II
TRUSTEES

2.1 Initial Trustee. JERRY SIMONS shall serve as the initial Trustee of this Trust.

2.2 Successor Trustees. If JERRY SIMONS ceases to serve as Trustee for any reason, then MARY SIMONS shall serve as successor Trustee. If MARY SIMONS is unable or unwilling to serve, then FIRST NATIONAL BANK shall serve as successor Trustee.

ARTICLE III
BENEFICIARIES

3.1 Primary Beneficiaries. The primary beneficiaries of this Trust shall be JERRY SIMONS during his lifetime, and upon his death, his spouse MARY SIMONS.

3.2 Contingent Beneficiaries. If MARY SIMONS predeceases JERRY SIMONS or disclaims her interest, the contingent beneficiaries shall be the children of JERRY SIMONS, namely JOHN SIMONS and JANE SIMONS, in equal shares.

ARTICLE IV
DISTRIBUTIONS

4.1 During Grantor's Lifetime. During the lifetime of the Grantor, the Trustee shall pay to or apply for the benefit of the Grantor all of the net income from the Trust Estate, and such portions of the principal as the Grantor may from time to time direct.

4.2 Upon Grantor's Death. Upon the death of the Grantor, the Trustee shall distribute the remaining Trust Estate to the beneficiaries as specified in Article III.

ARTICLE V
TRUST TYPE AND PURPOSE

5.1 Revocable Trust. This Trust is a revocable trust. The Grantor reserves the right to amend or revoke this Trust in whole or in part at any time.

5.2 Purpose. The purpose of this Trust is to provide for the management and distribution of the Grantor's assets during the Grantor's lifetime and upon the Grantor's death, and to avoid probate of the Trust Estate.

ARTICLE VI
TRUSTEE POWERS

The Trustee shall have all powers granted by applicable law, including but not limited to the power to invest and reinvest Trust assets, to buy, sell, lease, or mortgage property, to distribute income and principal, and to do all acts necessary for the proper administration of this Trust.

ARTICLE VII
MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

7.1 Governing Law. This Trust shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

7.2 Situs. The situs of this Trust shall be Los Angeles County, California.

7.3 Spendthrift Provision. No beneficiary may assign, transfer, or encumber their interest in this Trust, and no creditor of a beneficiary may attach or otherwise reach the beneficiary's interest.

7.4 Trustee Compensation. The Trustee shall be entitled to reasonable compensation for services rendered.

7.5 No Bond Required. No bond shall be required of any Trustee serving hereunder.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Trust Agreement on the date first written above.
